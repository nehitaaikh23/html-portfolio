JODIES Top Three Films of All Time

The Great Gatsby

This is my favorite movie of all time. Great and compelling story with amazing acting from Leonardo Di Caprio and Toby Maguire.

Inception

Another great movie starring Leonardo Di Caprio. Amazing story by Cristopher Nolan.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Amazing movie about the story of Jordan Belfort. Another movie starring Leonado Di Caprio. Jonah Hill and Margot Robbie did brilliantly.
